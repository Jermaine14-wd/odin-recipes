Honey Garlic Chicken

Description

This sauce not only tastes amazing, it's incredible satisfying watching it bubble and morph into a perfectly well-balanced savoury, sweet and garlicky syrup. But your mouth really begins to water watching the sauce simmer all around your chicken and coat it so effortlessly.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 6 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt, pepper and garlic powder; set aside

  2. Heat a pan or skillet over medium high heat; sear chicken thigh fillets or breast fillets on both sides until golden and cooked through.

    3. Sauce

  3. Sauce: Add garlic between the chickken and fry until fragrant. Add honey, water, vinegar and soy sauce. Increase heat to medium-high and continue to cook until the sauce reduces down and thicken slightly.

  4. Garish with parsley and serve over vegetables, rice, pasta or with a salad.
